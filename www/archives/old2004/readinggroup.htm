Reading Group

Beginnning this February, SOCS will be holding a reading group.  An interesting and poingnat paper in the fields related to Cognitive Science will be posted here each month and participants may download the paper and read it over the course of the month.  At the end of the month, an informal gathering will take place during which participants can discuss their opinions of the paper, related topics, and possibly hear from a member of the faculty on the issues raised in the paper.
