Welcome to the webpage of the Student Organization for Cognitive Science (SOCS)! This organization aims to provide a community for the Cog Sci students of Indiana University by putting together events and providing a place for discussions. We sponsor events such as movie nights with food and discussion, parties, lab tours, and reading groups. There is a lot planned for Spring semester 2004, so check out the events page for upcoming dates!

If you have any articles or books to suggest for the reading group go ahead and start a thread on the message board!

If you are interested in touring a lab, or showing off your own, post that on the message board as well!
