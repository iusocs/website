Welcome

Welcome to the webpage of the Student Organization for Cognitive Science (SOCS)! This organization aims to provide a community for the Cog Sci students of Indiana University by putting together events and providing a place for discussions. We organize events such as movie nights, lab tours, reading groups, and faculty dinners. There is a lot planned for 2007-2008, so check out the events page for upcoming dates! Also, don't forget to check out the Resources section for useful links and interesting dates.

To sign up for our mailing list, send a message to listserv@listserv.indiana.edu with a message containing "subscribe socs-l" as the body. You'll receive a confirmation email shortly thereafter.
