Resources

The following are sites we think you'll enjoy:

  • Cognoscente mailing list - The cognitive science mailing list for meetings, conferences, and colloquia. You should sign up for this mailing list.
  • IU Cognitive Science News - The departmental blog containing posts about a number of topics of interest to students.
  • IU Cognitive Science Program - The official site of Indiana University's cognitive science program.
  • IU Cognitive Science Undergraduate Site - The official undergrad cognitive science site.
  • Indiana Archives of Cognitive Science - Provides information about "the field of cognitive science including the history, current research trends, and career opportunities associated with the field."
  • Indiana Undergraduate Journal of Cognitive Science - A journal of research in cognitive science done by undergraduates at IU.
  • Indiana University Robotics Club - The official homepage for the robotics club!
