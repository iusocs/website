about

We are a community of cognitive science students at IU that attend and organize various events out of a passion for cognitive science or any field related to it.

Weekly Discussions

Tuesday's

@ 5pm

Psych building, room 128

Every Tuesday, anyone is invited to attend our weekly discussions and lectures with faculty and/or grad students about their research here at IU or elsewhere. Topics include computer science, linguistics, philosophy, psychology, neuroscience, artificial intelligence, or any possible combination of such as long as it's related to cognitive science in any way. Oh, and there's free food.

SOCS is a great way for anyone interested in cognitive science or research to network with other students and faculty and find out what area of cognitive science is most engaging for you. Our weekly meetings demonstrate just how fascinating and broad cognitive science can be, so even if you have the slightest interest in cognitive science or want to major in it but you aren't sure, come to a meeting!
